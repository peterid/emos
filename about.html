emOS is a speculative project exploring how emotionally intelligent systems could be used to manipulate employees into suppressing their emotions, for the sake of a happy and productive workplace. emOS prides itself in being a system which is there to combat sadness and depression in the office. However, the reality is a system which incorrectly analyses the user's emotion, trapping them in an endless loop of therapy, encouraging them to take pills which mask their real emotions, as it restricts people who it deems emotionally unstable for work.
